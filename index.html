Masyu

A Ludum Dare 33 game

Masyu is a puzzle game where the object is to create a single, continuous, non-intersecting loop that passes through all the special spaces. A line can enter and exit a grid cell in one of the four cardinal directions.

There are two types of special spaces: white and black:

White

A white space must be traveled straight through, but must turn in the previous and/or the next cell in its path.

Black

A black space must have a turn on their grid cell, but both the next and previous cells must have been traveled straight through.
